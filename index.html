SISU GLOBAL HEALTH

Medical Technology company focused on Sub-Saharan Africa

SOLVING GLOBAL HEALTH PROBLEMS WITH PRACTICAL TECHNOLOGY

Sisu Global Health seeks to end healthcare disparities by developing medical technology that addresses critical needs in developing countries. Our portfolio of products addresses needs in maternal/infant health and blood safety. Each of our devices are patent-pending and address the issues of access to medical devices by considering each stakeholder throughout the value chain.
